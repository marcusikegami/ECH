A New Standard
in Surgical Support

Extended Coverage Healthcare was built to solve one of surgical medicine's most persistent challenges: consistent, qualified assist coverage in the operating room. Our unique staffing model provides reliable case coverage so that surgeons, hospitals, and practices can focus entirely on patient outcomes.

We work across a wide spectrum of settings — from large hospital systems seeking to make their ORs more attractive to community surgeons, to private practices that need an APP available for both surgical coverage and office follow-up.

"All of these scenarios free up time and capital to allocate to patient care and other service areas."

Who We Serve

Our model is built for flexibility — designed to integrate seamlessly into hospital systems, ambulatory surgery centers, and independent surgical practices.

Hospitals

Utilize our model to make your ORs more attractive to community surgeons by including a floating CSA/APP — creating a more convenient and efficient surgical environment.

  • — Floating CSA/APP coverage
  • — Surgeon retention & recruitment
  • — Reduced staffing overhead

Surgery Centers

ASCs benefit from consistent assist coverage that keeps cases moving efficiently, reducing delays and creating a more attractive environment for your surgical volume.

  • — Consistent case coverage
  • — Reduced scheduling complexity
  • — Improved OR throughput

Surgical Practices

Have an APP/CSA designated for surgery while keeping an APP in the office for patient follow-up. ECH can also hire your current APPs and assign them back to you.

  • — Dedicated surgical APP
  • — Office follow-up coverage
  • — APP hire-back arrangements
